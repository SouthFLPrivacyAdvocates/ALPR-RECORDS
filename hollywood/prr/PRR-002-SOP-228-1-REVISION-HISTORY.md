## Request

Pursuant to Chapter 119, Florida Statutes, I am requesting copies of the following records:

**All prior versions of Hollywood Police Department Standard Operating Procedure 228.1, "License Plate Readers (LPR)," including the original version dated October 12, 2010 and every intervening revision issued before the current May 15, 2025 revision.**

If the department's policy management system (PowerDMS or equivalent) retains revision metadata, I also request the effective date and approving authority for each version.

If no prior versions exist in any retained form, I request written confirmation of that fact.

---

## Scope Limitation

This request is limited to the revision history of a **single already-identified document** (SOP 228.1). I am not requesting:

- Other HPD general orders, SOPs, or directives
- Draft or unapproved versions
- Training materials, operational logs, query records, or plate-read data
- Correspondence about the policy

The current (May 15, 2025) version has already been produced under P064086-040126 and is not re-requested here.

---

## Format

Electronic copies (PDF preferred) are acceptable. If revisions are archived in PowerDMS, a PowerDMS export of the version history for SOP 228.1 would satisfy this request.

---

## Basis

The May 15, 2025 revision of SOP 228.1, produced in response to P064086-040126, indicates an origination date of October 12, 2010. That origination date predates the earliest Hollywood City Commission authorization for ALPR equipment (R-2014-038, February 19, 2014) by more than three years. The policy's revision history is the most direct record of when and how the department's ALPR governance framework developed relative to its operational deployment, vendor changes (Motorola Solutions and Flock Safety are both named in the current revision), and the November 13, 2024 issuance of the Florida CJJIS Council's statewide ALPR guidelines.
