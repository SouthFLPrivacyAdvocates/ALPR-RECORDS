WHAT THIS INVESTIGATION REVEALS
================================================================================

The City of Plantation, FL has implemented a MULTI-FACETED AUTOMATED LICENSE 
PLATE READER (ALPR) surveillance infrastructure comprising:

1. MOBILE UNITS
   - 2 LPR speed trailers (deployed 2017)
   - Vendor: Vetted Security Solutions
   - Cost: $79,410 (Impact Fees)
   
2. PARK DEPLOYMENTS  
   - LPR cameras at 2 city parks
   - Funded by federal JAG grant: $21,743
   - Implemented with Broward Sheriff's Office
   
3. CORRIDOR SYSTEMS
   - SR7/Gateway Corridor ALPR (operational FY2024)
   - Midtown District ALPR (FY2024)
   - Funding: Special District revenue
   
4. CENTRALIZED DATA PLATFORM
   - Vigilant Solutions database
   - 24+ million monthly detections
   - Annual cost: $5,300 (ongoing, FY2021-2023 documented)

5. OPERATIONS EXPANSION (FY2024)
   - New "Digital Records and License Plate Recognition (LPR) Coordinator" position
   - Indicates system enhancement and increased operational scope
